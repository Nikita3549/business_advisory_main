Services for particular customers

We help both foreign and national entrepreneurs to open and sustain business in Spain.

1.Services for particular customers

  • Obtaining the NIE (foreigner identification number) for individuals
  • Digital signature (remote) for individuals
  • Digital signature (remote) for legal entities
  • Insurance (business activity, vehicles, health, etc.)
  • Donations and Inheritance
  • Purchase/sale of real estate (only in Torrevieja – Alicante area)
  • Long and short term rentals of real estate (only in the Torrevieja – Alicante area)
  • Change of vehicle ownership
  • Digital services (marketing, development, etc.)

2.Consulting in international trade

  • Obtaining the VAT number (VIES) and EORI
  • Online stores and distant sales IOSS
  • Procedures for intra-community sales and customs procedures for import/export to third countries
  • International transport organization (minimum 1 container)

3.Fast company formation

Creation of limited companies in 48 hours. We will assist you with:
  • Obtaining the NIF (tax identification number) for legal entities
  • Creation of limited companies
  • Accounting + taxes for limited companies
  • Liquidation of limited companies
  • Self-employed registration
  • License for the start of urban activity (bar, restaurant) or industrial activity (production, factories)

4.Labour law and HR

  • Preparation of employment contracts according to your needs
  • Payroll
  • Dismissals, disputes
  • Calculation and negotiation of severance
  • Social security contributions, ERTEs
  • Office hours time management and their correct registration
  • Prevention of occupational risks
  • Costs saving measures

5.Tax compliance

  • Corporate Tax (form 200)
  • Corporate Tax (form 202)
  • VAT declaration (form 303)
  • Income tax declaration (form 130/131)
  • Income tax declaration (form 100)
  • Non-Resident Income Tax (IRNR)
  • Information declaration on assets abroad (form 720)
  • Wealth Tax (form 714)
  • Management of Tax Agency requirements
  • Other tax returns
  • Non-Resident Income Tax (form 210)
  • Tax deferral
  • Form 600 for the purchase of real estate, loans, etc.

6.Accounting

  • Accounting + taxes for self-employed
  • Cost calculation, analysis
  • Business intelligence
  • Cuadros de mandos
  • Due diligence
  • Audit
  • Legalization of accounting books in the Commercial Registry
  • CRM and moblie app for companies and billing
